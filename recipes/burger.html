Burger Recipe

Description

Root beer and pulled pork might not seem like a likely pairing but here it makes all the difference. Topped with your favorite BBQ sauce, it's sure to bring rave reviews.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pound Tenderloin
  • 8 Hamburger buns
  • 1 Bottle of barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1
    Gather the ingredients
  2. Step 2
    Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top.
  3. Step 3
    Cover and cook on Low until pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours. Note: the actual length of time may vary according to the individual slow cooker
  4. Step 4
    Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce.
  5. Step 5
    Serve on hamburger buns.
  6. Step 6
    Serve and enjoy!
